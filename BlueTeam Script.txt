Caldera script-

The Dre box is our blue team box. The Dre box comes equipped with some familiar tools such as wireshark
to sort through traffic to find potential suspicious acitivty in the network. It also comes with a new tool
called "Caldera". Caldera is a sandbox framework that can be customized and used to simulate offensive and defensive attacks. 
This can be a valuable training tool, or be used to test defenses of a system. *Open Caldera and shoe the different options
for both red and blue team.
